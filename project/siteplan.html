Insurance Awareness - Site Plan

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Insurance Awareness website is to educate working adults and young families about the value of financial protection through funeral cover, life cover, and accidental cover. It aims to explain these insurance concepts in plain language, supported by visuals, comparisons, and an interactive premium calculator, helping users choose the right coverage for their needs.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A 35-year-old working parent in Zimbabwe wants to understand life cover to secure their family’s future. They visit the site to read the overview and use the premium calculator to estimate costs.
  • Scenario 2: A young couple, aged 28, needs to decide between funeral and accidental cover after a friend’s recent loss. They explore the comparison table on the Funeral & Life Cover page to make an informed choice.

Site Name and Rationale

Site Name: "Insurance Awareness"
Reason: This name clearly reflects the site’s focus on educating users about insurance options, making it accessible and relevant to individuals seeking to understand and plan their financial protection.

Color Scheme

  • Color 1: #2c3e50 (Dark Slate Blue) - Used for headings and key text to convey trust and professionalism.
  • Color 2: #e67e22 (Orange) - Applied to buttons, accents, and call-to-action elements to draw attention and encourage engagement.

Typography

Font: Inter (weights: 400, 500, 700)
Application: Inter will be used for all text, with 400 for body content, 500 for subheadings, and 700 for main headings, ensuring clarity and readability across the site’s educational content and interactive tools.

Home Page Wireframes

Mobile Wireframe

Description: A single-column layout for screens up to 640px, featuring a header with the site title, a hero section with an introduction, a hamburger menu, and cards for funeral, life, and accidental cover overviews, ending with a call-to-action button.

Wider View Wireframe

Description: A two-column layout for screens 1025px and above, with a header, a full-width hero, a navigation bar, a grid of three cards for insurance types, and a prominent call-to-action, all optimized for desktop viewing.

Site Structure

  • Home Page – Guide to Insurance (Awareness) - Introduction to financial protection and brief overviews of funeral, life, and accidental cover.
  • Funeral & Life Cover - Detailed explanations, differences, and a comparison table of benefits and costs.
  • Accidental Cover - Information on accidental death/disability cover, examples, and an interactive premium calculator.
